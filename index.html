Raspberry Pi Time Lapse Camera

I wanted to be able to capture photos as frequently as possible of myself to track my movements and ageing over time.

I have in the past created a daemon that ran on my laptop and took photos using the webcam and this worked great but only worked when the laptop was on and only captured my face.

That’s what spurred me on to get a Raspberry Pi with a high resolution webcam to allow me to stick it in the corner of the room and to start taking pictures which would generate and upload a daily time lapse.

Behind the scenes it uses the fantastic motion library which makes it a doddle to configure your device to regularly take photos and conveniently creates time lapse videos for you.

Current Issues

I keep facing problems with photos just not being captured, with nothing in the logs that indicate something going wrong. I suspect this is a memory (RAM) issue and will run a cron to clear out memory caches.

I am also facing issues uploading the time lapse videos created, this could be a number of things but seems to be with the motion library on_video_created event.
